Biography

I am a seasoned technical writer and content strategist. I have written for developers as well as non-technical end users. I have specialized in building content delivery systems using a docs-as-code approach. I am passionate about getting the right content in front of the right people. I hold a master’s degree in Professional Writing from Northern Arizona University, studying information design and usability.

I am currently based in the suburbs of Phoenix, Arizona.
